「成语拼拼」致力于保护您的隐私。本应用采用“隐私至上”的设计理念，您的绝大部分数据均保存在您的本地设备上。我们不会出售您的数据，也不会将数据用于广告精准投放。

"Idiom Puzzle" is committed to protecting your privacy. We adopt a "Privacy First" design—most of your data stays on your local device. We do not sell your data or use it for targeted advertising.

Includes game progress, unlock status, and in-app preferences. This data is stored locally (e.g., iOS UserDefaults) to maintain a seamless user experience.

1.2 Automatically Collected Technical Information

为优化兼容性，应用可能会读取您的设备型号、系统版本号及崩溃日志。这些信息通常是去中心化的、匿名的，仅用于故障排除。

To optimize compatibility, the app may read your device model, OS version, and crash logs. This information is typically decentralized and anonymous, used solely for troubleshooting.

三、数据存储与安全性

3. Data Storage and Security

本地化：您的所有学习记录均存储在设备沙盒内。这意味着如果您卸载应用，相关本地数据将一并被系统删除。

Localization: All your records are stored within the device sandbox. Uninstalling the app will trigger the system to delete all associated local data.

无服务器传输：本应用不设后台服务器，不进行任何云端同步或个人敏感数据的网络传输。

No Server Transfer: This app has no backend server and performs no cloud synchronization or network transfer of sensitive personal data.

四、第三方共享

4. Third-Party Sharing

我们不接入任何第三方广告 SDK（如 AdMob）或行为追踪工具（如 Google Analytics）。我们不向任何第三方公司共享、租用或出售您的信息。

We do not integrate any third-party ad SDKs (e.g., AdMob) or tracking tools (e.g., Google Analytics). We do not share, rent, or sell your info to third parties.

五、儿童隐私特别保护

5. Special Protection for Children

本应用符合儿童应用隐私规范。我们不收集儿童的任何个人身份信息。我们鼓励家长陪同孩子学习，并建议家长利用系统内置的“屏幕使用时间”功能管理孩子的游戏时间。

This app complies with privacy standards for children's apps. We do not collect PII from children. We encourage parental guidance and recommend using "Screen Time" to manage usage.
